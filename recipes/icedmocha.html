Iced Mocha

Iced Mocha is easy to make if you can wait a day for it, but it's worth the wait for that cold drink on a hot day.

Ingrediants/h3>
  • 1 ½ cups of cold coffee
  • 2 cups of milk
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • ¼ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Pour coffee into ice cube tray. Freeze until solid, or overnight.
  2. In a blender, combine coffee ice cubes, milk, chocolate syrup and sugar. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
