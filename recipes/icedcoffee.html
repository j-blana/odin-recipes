Iced Coffee

A simple recipe to make the usual coffee that you drink cold, perfect for those that prefer cold drinks.

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups ice cubes, or as needed
  • 8 cups strong freshly brewed coffee
  • 1 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 4 cups whole milk

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups ice cubes to a 2-quart pitcher. Slowly pour coffee over ice; stir until ice is melted. Stir in sugar; mix thoroughly until completely dissolved. Add milk, stirring just until combined. Stir in remaining 2 cups ice, adding more as needed; refrigerate until well chilled, about 30 minutes.
  2. Stir before serving. Pour over ice in tall glasses or into recycled serving-size glass bottles or insulated tumblers.
